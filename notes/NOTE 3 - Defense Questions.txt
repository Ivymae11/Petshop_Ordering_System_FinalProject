DEFENSE QUESTIONS

1. What is the purpose of this system pair?
The PHP system manages the pet shop while the C# system allows customers to order pet products.

2. What database is used?
SQLite.

3. How are the systems connected?
The C# app sends HTTP requests to api.php, and api.php reads and writes the SQLite database.

4. What can the admin do?
Manage products, categories, suppliers, customers, orders, stock, and statuses.

5. What can the customer do?
Login, register, browse products, add to cart, checkout, view orders, and cancel allowed orders.

6. Why is stock validation needed?
It prevents customers from ordering more than the available stock.

7. What happens after checkout?
The order is saved, stock is deducted, and the order appears in the PHP admin system.

8. Why is the C# form fixed-size?
To keep buttons, tables, dropdowns, and textboxes consistently visible.
